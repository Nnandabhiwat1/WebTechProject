Hi friends!

The purpose of this website is to showcase the story of my life, my interests, and my hobbies through the lens of my unique skill-set and passion for film photography. It will serve as a platform for people to get to know me better, with a particular focus on my artwork. Through my photographs, I aim to express my gratitude for the people, places, and experiences that have shaped me, including my family, friends, travels, adventures, and scenic landscapes. My goal is to create a dynamic and engaging website that captures the essence of my life and experiences, both past and present. Ultimately, I hope this website will serve not only as a tool for appreciation but also as a kaleidoscope of cherished memories that have shaped me become the person I am today. As time in college passes quickly, I am committed to capturing as many memories as possible and remaining grateful for the incredible opportunities that lie ahead.
